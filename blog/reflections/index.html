    Where puppies grow

    Daunting desert hill
    Fly, blue butterfly, and drink
    Oasis flowers

    Fiery compost

    Soggy autumn leaves
    Freshly rotting on the heap
    Inside, chilling warmth

    Empire of liberty

    Time for your jab, sir

    Any strychnine left?

    Some like it hot

    Lines in the sand at
    Hotel del Coronado
    Washed out by the sun

    Headshot habit

    Care to stay alive?
    Forehead scan says red or green
    Sudden death — not yet
