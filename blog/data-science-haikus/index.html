    Five data science haikus

    By Frederik Durant February 15, 2015 Tweet Like +1

    One

    As data unfold
    Alas! The model won’t fit
    Still the curve bends right

    Two

    While frequentists count,
    Bayesians believe - or not
    Now what is my fate?

    Three

    Support my vectors
    Will you - the margin must grow
    Wheat from chaff apart

    Four

    The deeper we learn
    The lonelier the neurons
    Black magic at work

    Five

    My profile is me
    A bunch of features - that’s all
    But where is my soul?
