    On a data science sabbatical in NYC

    data science • new york

    Welcome

    By Frederik Durant December 26, 2014 Tweet Like +1

    It’s been almost half a decade since I wrote my last (public) blog post, so I figured it was high time for a restart.

    The occasion: two weeks from now, I’ll be travelling to New York for a 12-week Data Science Bootcamp organized by Metis. During that period, I’ll be living at the Brooklyn Krashpad, a co-housing facility for entrepreneurs, creative people and other types of lifelong learners and doers.

    Since visualization, presentation and communication form an integral part of data science, I plan to share my learnings, insights and other adventures via these pages.

    So thanks for visiting, and stay tuned!
