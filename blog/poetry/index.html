    Musings from a distant present

    By Frederik Durant July 29, 2015 Tweet Like +1

    Memories

    Through the veil of time
    Distant features once so close
    Glimmer on the mind

    City walls

    High on the rooftops
    Water towers bleed to death
    Staining the brownstones

    Broken light

    On the window sill
    The glass shines, filled with moonbeams
    scattered on the floor

    Winter

    Snowflake by snowflake
    Early spring’s nipped in the bud
    Flowers in the clouds

    Moments

    From tomorrow on
    Yesterday won’t be the same
    as it was today
