    Five hypekus — because we can

    By Frederik Durant January 20, 2018 Tweet Like +1

    Artificial intelligence

    Knowing humankind’s
    Usual intelligence
    Can AI do worse?

    Blockchain

    When the snake is forked
    And schismatizes in two
    What is fool’s truth worth?

    Crypto-currency

    Them coins and tokens
    Soaped up in bubbly wallets
    Yearning for value

    Deep learning

    Valley in sight, when
    Selection bias abyss
    Swallows the climber

    Internet of things

    On the Internet
    Each dog knows a smelly thing
    To sniff that’s secret
